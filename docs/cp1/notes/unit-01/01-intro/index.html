Lesson 01 - Introduction to Computer Science

What is Computer Science?



Computer Science is …

  • art : “An expression or application of human creative skill and imagination”
  • science : “The intellectual and practical activity encompassing the systematic study of the structure and behavior of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment.” and also, “a systematically organized body of knowledge on a particular subject”
  • engineering : “Application of knowledge in order to invent, design, build, maintain, and improve structures, machines, devices, systems, materials and processes.”


Computer Science as a field includes the following components:

  • Theoretical CS is the study of abstract and mathematical aspects of computing.
  • Applied CS is the practice of using computers to solve real-world problems.
  • Computer scientists must also consider Social and Ethical issues related to computers and computing.


Computer Programming is a critical tool in Applied CS.

  • Computer Programming, broadly speaking, includes the design and creation of computer programs.


Why Computer Science?

A few reasons …

  • Computers are becoming increasingly entwined with our lives.
  • Many computer scientists find their jobs to be meaningful and personally fulfilling.
  • The pay and the job outlook are great!

Visual Programming

Visual programming editors use blocks which are like puzzle-pieces that click together to determine how a program works.

The following were mostly developed by Google and/or MIT

  • Blockly games / demos
    • “Blockly is a web-based, graphical programming editor. Users can drag blocks together to build an application. No typing required.”
  • Scratch / Snap!
    • “Scratch is a free programming language and online community where you can create your own interactive stories, games, and animations.”
    • “[Snap is an extension of Scratch…] suitable for a serious introduction to computer science for high school or college students.”
